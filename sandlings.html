sandlings

The whole world is physical pixel sand. Sandlings pour out of the portal and march blindly — walls turn them around, water drowns them, deep falls hurt them. Get enough of them to the exit door.

Tilt your phone to shift sand and water across the screen. Desktop: arrow keys tilt gravity. Draw with sand / water, or dig tunnels with your finger or mouse. Gear stations (snorkel, axe, umbrella) upgrade every sandling that walks past. Every level is procedurally generated — no two are the same.
