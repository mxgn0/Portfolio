rennings

The whole world is physical pixel sand. Rennings pour out of the portal and march blindly — walls turn them around, deep water drowns them, falls hurt them, lava burns them. Get enough of them to the exit door.

Tilt your phone to shift sand, snow, water and lava across the screen (desktop: arrow keys) — the spirit level up top shows how far the world is tipped. Your sand & water supply is limited — tilting the world is free. Water quenches lava into stone and puts out fires; sand poured into lava becomes a stone bridge. Dig tunnels with your finger. Rennings skate helplessly across ice when the world tips, and boats only really move when you tilt — ferry them across. Sand and snow heap up into steep little piles as you tip the world; water sloshes into crests and settles flat again when you hold still. Path ready? Hit 2× to fast-forward the march. On their own the rennings won't make it — every obstacle hides its matching gear in a supply crate somewhere earlier on the path: snorkel, boat, axe, lighter, umbrella, climbing hook, bridge planks. Some crates sit on rock ledges or a floor above — build a sand ramp and march a renning past to claim them; sometimes the boat waits up on the mountain. Then place the gear where the crowd needs it; every renning that walks past picks it up. Bonus crates pay out extra sand, water or statues. Falls kill quickly without an umbrella. From lvl 2 on levels mix three obstacle types, lvl 3+ even more — and the dice decide how big a forest, lake or lava pool gets. Some levels drown a whole floor under one giant lake, forest or frozen sea. Find hidden keys to open gates — even floor exits can be locked, and a key is never anywhere near its door. On roundtrip levels the exit sits right next to the spawn — locked. The key waits at the far end of the world, beside a portal home: the whole level is the detour. Meadow, jungle, mountain and cave worlds — some levels are pitch black night. Place a few turn-around statues to steer the crowd — and don't let anyone run head-on into a torch carrier. Higher floors must be unlocked by clearing the ones below, and pay more points. Every level is procedurally generated: score, stars & streaks live on the leaderboard.
