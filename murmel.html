murmel

A marble in a maze — and gravity is your only controller. Tilt your phone to roll it (desktop: arrow keys / WASD, or hold the mouse — the marble rolls toward it). The bubble level up top shows your tilt.

Every maze is procedurally generated and full of the same matter the rennings world is made of: sand slows you down, on ice you skate with zero grip, lava melts marbles, and holes swallow them — roll fast and you can shoot right across a hole. Grab the coins, touch checkpoints to save your spot, and reach the orange goal ring. You have three marbles. Bigger mazes must be unlocked by clearing the ones before — score, stars & streaks live on the leaderboard.
